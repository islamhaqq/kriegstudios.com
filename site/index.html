What is Krieg Studios?

Krieg Studios is a game developer. We consist of brilliant and enthusiastic engineers, developers, artists, designers, leaders, writers, and multi-talented individuals. We work together to create magnificent projects to amaze the world.

Why Virtual Reality?

At this moment KS is making a virtual reality videogame. VR is about to explode like a supervolcano within the next two years. We want to get something out there right on time. Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Samsung, HTC, Sony, Valve, Qualcomm, Oculus, and tons of other companies are investing billions of dollars into VR. The VR industry is expected exponentially grow to $38,000,000,000 by 2020. To put this in perspective, the Smartphone industry is around this number in 2016. Augmented reality is also similar and big (Pokemon Go). There is a lot to expect from VR/AR, so Krieg Studios will invest its resouces into this market for a while.

What does Krieg mean?

Krieg means "war" in German. The original game idea was a team-oriented World War II real time strategy game called "Krieg," but that would have been a very difficult endevour even with a large team. So Krieg was abandoned. The name stuck, Krieg Studios was formed, and now KS has moved onto even greater things. Currently KS is working on a virtual reality game instead of the much more arduos RTS game, but who knows what the future beholds.
